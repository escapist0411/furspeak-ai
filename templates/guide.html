Dog Emotion Guide 🐶

😊 Happy

Dogs bark happily when excited or playing. Signs: • Wagging tail • Jumping • Playful bark

😡 Angry

Dogs growl when feeling threatened. Signs: • Teeth showing • Loud growling • Defensive stance

😢 Sad

Dogs whine when lonely or sad. Signs: • Low energy • Whining • Avoiding eye contact

🍖 Hungry

Dogs bark repeatedly when hungry. Signs: • Sitting near food area • Repeated barking
